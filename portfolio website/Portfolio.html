ACTIVITIES of SUMMER 2017

Computer Science

Computer Science is a new adventure that I have embarked on in the summer of 2017. I was never a tech person but in this day in age of rapid technological advancement, it is most crucial to have a basic understanding of it all. Currently, I am a participant of the Girls Who Code program at Adobe, NY. This program allowed me to dip my toe in the field of computer science. During the program I completed many projects(including creating this portfolio website), which are displayed in the picture gallary to the left.

City Council

Politics started becoming more pronounced in my life beginning with the 2017 presidential elections. Being a first generation Asian American, U.S. politics, or politics in general, was never the center of discussion, thus, all the information that I recieved about current events or world events came from school discussion or news articles. This summer I became involved in City Council Elections. I work as a volunteer where I learn and participate in the different stages of the election.
